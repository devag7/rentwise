# Task starter — brand photography generation

For gpt-image-2 or similar. Use when generating lifestyle/touchpoint imagery.

## Master prompt template

> Documentary photo, [SUBJECT], real Bangalore apartment interior, soft window light, neutral color cast with warm shadows, visible film grain, slight clutter (books, plants, charging cables), editorial composition with negative space, subject mid-task not looking at camera. 4:3.

Vary subjects across a set; name a specific ethnicity per prompt (Black, East Asian, Latina, South Asian, Middle Eastern, mixed-race) and vary body types — South Asian-majority casting fits the Bangalore market, but never an all-same-cast grid.

## Subject substitutes
Instead of "person doing X at laptop": person taping a moving box, comparing two phones on a balcony, measuring a wall, holding chai while reading a lease on paper, pinning a map of Bangalore.

## Avoid in prompts
"professional", "premium", "studio lighting", "stock photo", glass coworking spaces, "engineers at laptops", skyline-at-dusk, handshake, keys-in-palm, perfect symmetry, gradient backgrounds.

## Banned concepts
Hourglasses, lightbulbs, network nodes, glowing brains, upward-arrow cityscapes, house-shaped clip art.

## Quality bar
Film grain always. Imperfect light. Found, not staged. If it looks AI-smooth — regenerate.

## No-text guardrail (append verbatim)
> No text, no letters, no numbers, no watermarks, no logos, no signage with readable words anywhere in the image.

Never name real publications (Vogue, TIME, etc.) in prompts — they bake in mastheads.
